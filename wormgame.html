Funny Worm Game

Funny Worm Game is a 2D physics platformer in which the player controls a worm to maneuver around the level, grabbing, eating, and jumping as they go. It was made for my Game Studio 1 class with a team of seven people over a three week period.

Features/Things I built:

  • Player controller creation
  • Animation rigging
  • Physics and controls
  • Level progression
  • UI/UX implementation
