Demetri Nekos: Game Programmer

About Me

Hello! I am a Game Programmer studying at Champlain College! I enjoy building game systems and tools as well as narrative integration in games. At Champlain I learned how to work in large teams with agile and Scrum methodologies. I even became a certified ScrumMaster.

I started my interest in game development almost immediatly after I started to learn how to code. I grew up on games and want to pursue the development of my programming skills in an artistically and creatively fulfilling way. Now, I have made multiple games as part of my coursework and I enjoy participating in game jams with a team or solo.

Outside of game development, I try to keep myself busy. I enjoy hiking and camping, as well as reading. As a kid I was apart of my local scouting organization, and achieved the rank of Eagle Scout; I also gained a black belt in karate, and am an amateur sabre fencer.
