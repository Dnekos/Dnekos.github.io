Lucifur: The Witch's Familiar

Lucifur is a top down twin stick action game about a witch's familiar cleaning up her lair by extinguishing candles. As candles are extinguished, ghosts come out and take over the once lit areas. Lucifur must extinguish the candles, avoid the ghosts, and make it out! This game was made as part of Abertay University's Game Developer Socieity's 2021 Halloween Game Jame, with a team of six people over the course of a weekend. You can play it online on itch.io

Features/Things I built:

  • Taught and assisted newer teammates on Unity
  • Player interactions
  • Enemy AI state machines and pathfinding
